# Capitalization Rules

Rule group: `CAPITALIZATION`
Category weight: 10 pts

---

## Rules

### CA-01 — First word of sentence not capitalized
**Severity:** major  
**Pattern:** Sentence begins with a lowercase letter (excluding intentional lowercase brand names like `iPhone`)  
**Example:**
- ❌ `chúng tôi đang có ưu đãi mới.`
- ✅ `Chúng tôi đang có ưu đãi mới.`

### CA-02 — Proper noun not capitalized (English)
**Severity:** major  
**Pattern:** Known proper nouns (country names, city names, person names) written in lowercase in English text  
**Example:**
- ❌ `visit us in ho chi minh city`
- ✅ `Visit us in Ho Chi Minh City`

### CA-03 — Vietnamese proper noun capitalization
**Severity:** minor  
**Pattern:** In Vietnamese, each word in a proper noun should be capitalized  
**Example:**
- ❌ `thành phố hồ chí minh`
- ✅ `Thành phố Hồ Chí Minh`
- **Note:** Capitalization rules in Vietnamese are less standardized than English — flag as `minor`, not `major`.

### CA-04 — ALL CAPS overuse
**Severity:** suggestion  
**Pattern:** More than 3 consecutive ALL-CAPS words (excluding hashtags and legal disclaimers)  
**Example:**
- ❌ `MUA NGAY ĐỂ NHẬN ƯU ĐÃI ĐẶC BIỆT HÔM NAY`
- Suggestion: Use standard capitalization; all-caps feels like shouting.

### CA-05 — Brand name capitalization
**Severity:** major  
**Pattern:** Known brands written with wrong capitalization  
**Examples:**
- ❌ `iphone` → `iPhone`
- ❌ `facebook` → `Facebook`
- ❌ `tiktok` → `TikTok`
- ❌ `youtube` → `YouTube`
- ❌ `shopee` → `Shopee`
- ❌ `lazada` → `Lazada`

### CA-06 — Day/month names in English
**Severity:** minor  
**Pattern:** Days of week or month names in English not capitalized  
**Example:**
- ❌ `Sale on monday and tuesday`
- ✅ `Sale on Monday and Tuesday`
